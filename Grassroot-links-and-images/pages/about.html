About Page

Grassroot Academy is a free online learning platform that empowers aspiring web developers to learn together.
It offers a Foundations Course that teaches the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
The course is designed for beginners and is completely free. The course covers topics such as how to create links to pages on other websites on the internet,
how to create links to other pages on your own websites, the difference between absolute and relative links,
and how to display an image on a webpage using HTML
